Typeface helps you pick the perfect font from your collection with a minimal interface focused entirely on your fonts. Typography is beautiful and essential to design, yet finding the right one can be challenging. Enter any text to preview and explore the delicate curves typographers craft.

Perfectly Rendered Previews

Quick Collection and Advanced Tools

How It Works

1. Browse Your Fonts

Open Typeface to see all installed fonts or load from a directory. Use list or grid view, with custom preview text and sizes tailored to your needs.

2. Preview and Compare

Enter text, adjust settings like spacing, ligatures, or metrics. Compare fonts side-by-side with overlays to spot nuances instantly.

3. Build Pairings and Layouts

Command-K to open Pairing Studio. Drag fonts, customize text blocks with presets, duplicate for iterations, and refine with version history.

4. Export and Activate

Print layouts, export PDFs, or activate fonts directly. Save to Quick Collection for future reference.
